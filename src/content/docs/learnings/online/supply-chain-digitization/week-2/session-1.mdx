## Context

Week 2 focuses on **Supply Chain Segmentation**. Before segmentation can be understood, we must understand *why* it is needed — i.e., what problems it solves.

<Aside type="note">
Common theme: **Digitization = Data Collection. Analytics = Data Usage → Better SC decisions.**
</Aside>

---

## Challenge 1: Demand Forecasting

**Core Problem:** Very difficult to accurately predict customer requirements.
Inaccurate forecasting → **Overstocking OR Stockouts** — both harm SC performance.

**Example:** A clothing retailer overstocks on winter clothing due to an inaccurate forecast → excess inventory → higher inventory cost → reduced profit margins.

**Digitization Solution:**
- RFID and real-time data collection tools → capture live demand signals
- Advanced Analytics → analyze historical data + market trends
- Machine Learning / Predictive Analytics → accurately forecast demand → better inventory decisions

<Aside type="caution">
Demand forecasting is the root challenge — it cascades into inventory and all other decisions.
</Aside>

---

## Challenge 2: Inventory Management

**Core Problem:** The goal is to match inventory closely with demand.
- Too much inventory → high holding cost
- Too little inventory → stockout

**Examples:**
- Popular consumer electronics: high demand underestimated → stockout → competitors launch alternatives → losses
- Less popular product: excess inventory carried → product unsold → high carrying cost

**Digitization Solution:**
- IoT devices + RFID technology → digital inventory tracking systems → real-time visibility
- Data Analytics: optimize stock levels, identify slow-moving items, flag items needing JIT practices

<Aside type="note">
IoT + RFID → real-time visibility → smarter inventory decisions.
</Aside>

---

## Challenge 3: Supplier Relationship Management

**Core Problem:** SC is dependent on partners, especially suppliers. High dependency on limited suppliers = high disruption risk. If even one supplier fails → entire SC gets disrupted.

**Example (Automotive):** Automotive manufacturer facing production delays. Root cause: key supplier unable to meet quality standards → temporary halt in manufacturing.

**Digitization Solution:**
- Digital platforms integrated with suppliers → track performance in real time
- Data Analytics: assess supplier performance, track lead time, evaluate risk factors
- Always maintain alternative suppliers to handle disruptions

---

## Challenge 4: Transportation & Logistics Delays

**Core Problem:** Capacity constraints, rising transport costs, and delivery delays — all directly impact SC efficiency.

**Example (Shipping):** Shipping company delivering to production facility. Port strike → delivery delay → manufacturing schedule disrupted.

**Digitization Solution:**
- GPS tracking → real-time monitoring and large data collection
- Route planning and route optimisation → streamline operations
- Data Analytics: optimise routes, predict vehicle maintenance needs, enhance overall logistics efficiency

---

## Challenge 5: Technology Integration

**Core Problem:** Integrating IoT + AI + Blockchain into SC operations. Compatibility issues with existing software hinder real-time data flow.

**Example:** Company implementing IoT-based tracking system. New system incompatible with existing software → real-time data flow disrupted.

**Digitization Solution:**
- Digital platforms → enable technology integration
- Data Analytics → analyze large volumes of data from integrated technologies → develop actionable insights

---

## Challenge 6: Regulatory Compliance

**Core Problem:** Complex SC processes must follow regulatory requirements. Non-compliance → delayed shipments and fines.

**Example (Pharma):** Pharma company facing new labeling regulations. Non-compliance → product shipment delays and potential fines.

**Digitization Solution:**
- Digital platforms → automate monitoring of compliances
- Integrate relevant regulations directly into SC processes
- Data Analytics → continuously monitor critical compliance parameters → alert system on deviations

---

## Challenge 7: Sustainability & Environmental Concerns

**Core Problem:** Companies must meet sustainability goals (carbon, waste, energy). Failure → reputational damage and regulatory scrutiny.

**Example (Food & Beverage):** Company facing resistance from environmental groups due to excessive packaging → reputational damage and potential legal scrutiny.

**Digitization Solution:**
- Digital platforms + IoT sensors → monitor sustainability indicators: carbon emissions, energy consumption, waste reduction
- Data Analytics → analyze environmental impact → strategize to meet sustainability targets

---

## Challenge 8: Cybersecurity Threats

**Core Problem:** As data flows across the SC → sensitive data protection becomes critical. Cyber threats can lead to data breaches, customer trust loss, financial loss, and legal consequences.

**Example (Retailer):** Data breach in online platform → customer information exposed → loss of trust, financial losses, legal consequences.

**Digitization Solution:**
- Robust cybersecurity measures → control and protect processes
- Data Analytics → continuously monitor critical parameters
- ML algorithms → predict anomalies in network activity

---

## Challenge 9: Talent Shortage & Skills Gap

**Core Problem:** Finding and retaining skilled SC professionals is critical. A skills gap reduces the ability to optimize operations and respond to market changes.

**Example (Logistics):** Logistics company struggling to hire experienced SC analytics professionals → operations suffer.

**Digitization Solution:**
- Automation of routine tasks → frees time for SC professionals to focus on strategic decisions
- Data Analytics → identify skill gaps in workforce → enable targeted training programs

---

## Summary: SC Challenges & Solutions Framework

| Challenge | Key Risk | Digital/Analytics Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Demand Forecasting | Overstock / Stockout | RFID + ML / Predictive Analytics |
| Inventory Mgmt | High holding cost / Stockout | IoT + RFID + Inventory tracking |
| Supplier Mgmt | Disruption from single source | Digital platforms + Supplier analytics |
| Transport & Logistics | Delays, cost overruns | GPS tracking + Route optimisation |
| Tech Integration | Data flow disruption | Integrated digital platforms |
| Regulatory Compliance | Fines, shipment delays | Automated compliance monitoring |
| Sustainability | Reputational and regulatory risk | IoT sensors + Environmental analytics |
| Cybersecurity | Data breach, financial loss | Robust security + Anomaly detection ML |
| Talent / Skills Gap | Operational inefficiency | Automation + Training analytics |

<Aside type="note">
All nine challenges above lead to the need for **Supply Chain Segmentation** — covered in upcoming sessions of Week 2.
</Aside>
